Game of Life Puzzle

How to Play:

Goal: Predict the stable state of the Game of Life on the right panel.

Rules: Set the birth rule (exact number of neighbors needed) and survival rules (which neighbor counts allow survival).

Left Panel: Shows the initial state. You can step through evolution or jump ahead.

Right Panel: Click cells to create your prediction of the final stable state.

Submit: You can submit your answer at any time - the game will calculate the correct answer automatically!
